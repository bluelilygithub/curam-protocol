How RAG Search Works

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines semantic search with AI to deliver accurate, source-backed answers.

1

Semantic Search

Your question is converted to a vector embedding and matched against your document library using semantic similarity—not just keyword matching.

2

Context Retrieval

The most relevant document chunks are retrieved and ranked by relevance score. These become the context for the AI model.

3

Grounded Generation

The AI generates an answer using ONLY the retrieved context—it cannot hallucinate or invent information from the internet.

4

Source Citations

Every answer includes links to the source documents and page numbers, so your team can verify the information.
