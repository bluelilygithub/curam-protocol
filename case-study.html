The Challenge

A 70-person structural engineering firm was experiencing significant operational inefficiencies. Their Office Manager spent 23+ hours per month manually entering vendor invoices into Xero, while senior engineers wasted 45-60 minutes per schedule manually transcribing beam specifications from PDF drawings into Excel for capacity checking.

The firm's leadership recognized that these repetitive tasks were consuming valuable billable capacity, but they were skeptical about AI solutions. Previous attempts with generic automation tools had failed due to the unique variance in their engineering documents.

The Solution: Curam-Ai Protocol™

The firm engaged with Curam-Ai Protocol™ following our structured four-phase approach, ensuring risk mitigation and predictable outcomes at each stage.

1 Phase 1

Feasibility Sprint $1,500

The firm submitted 8 sample documents representing their full range of complexity (clean invoices, messy handwritten schedules, legacy drawings). Within 48 hours, we delivered a Validated Benchmark showing ≥92% accuracy across all critical fields. The test passed, unlocking Phase 2.

2 Phase 2

Readiness Roadmap $8,500

We conducted targeted interviews with the Office Manager, Drafting Manager, and senior engineers to map baseline workflows. The analysis revealed:

  • Invoice Processing: 350 invoices/month × 4.0 minutes = 23.3 hours/month wasted
  • Schedule Digitization: 12 major schedules/month × 50 minutes = 10 hours/month wasted
  • Total Admin Burden: 33.3 hours/month of senior staff time on non-billable tasks

The Board-Ready Business Case demonstrated a $214,500 gross return in Year 1 through capacity reallocation, risk mitigation, and efficiency gains.

3 Phase 3

Compliance Shield $6,500

We mapped the deployment against ISO 27001 controls and validated all data residency requirements. The solution was architected exclusively within their existing M365/Azure tenant, ensuring zero data egress and eliminating Shadow IT risk. The CTO approved the architecture within 2 weeks.

4 Phase 4

Wave 1 Implementation $28,500

Over 6 weeks, we deployed three core routines: Invoice Gatekeeper, Schedule Digitiser, and Automated Drawing Register. The solution integrated seamlessly with their existing Xero accounting system and SharePoint infrastructure. Full IP handover was completed, including source code, documentation, and training videos.
