We build a three-layer validation system to prevent costly errors:

Layer 1 - Confidence Scoring: If the AI's confidence score is below 90% on critical fields (portfolio balances, transaction amounts, risk profiles, client objectives), the document is automatically flagged for human review.

Layer 2 - Business Logic Validation: We configure rules specific to your requirements. Examples: "Portfolio balances must match platform totals," "Risk profiles must match client fact finds," "Transaction dates must be within statement periods." Mismatches trigger alerts.

Layer 3 - Human Review Queue: Flagged records route to your support staff or associates within the same workflow. Review takes 30-60 seconds to confirm or correct.

Target accuracy: 99%+ on production documents after model training. The small percentage flagged for review prevents catastrophic errors (incorrect portfolio balances, misclassified risk profiles, compliance violations).

System designed to "fail safe." Extractions below 95% confidence flagged for human review. Staff verify before commitment. You're eliminating typing, not eliminating oversight.
