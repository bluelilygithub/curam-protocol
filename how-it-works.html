The Journey of Risk: From Diagnostic to Deployment

We manage project uncertainty by defining clear commercial gates that ensure predictable value, data security, and full IP ownership before the final investment.

The Curam-Ai Protocol replaces uncertainty with discipline.

The Incremental Commitment Journey

The Protocol transforms AI adoption from a risk into a predictable, validated investment through four distinct, fixed-fee commercial gates.

PHASE 1

The Feasibility Sprint

(Prove It Before You Invest)

$1,500 | ~48 Hours

We take 5–10 of your messiest documents to prove the technical concept. This establishes the ≥90% Field-Level Accuracy benchmark required for every subsequent phase.

RISK SHARED:

100% Money-Back Guarantee if accuracy is not met.

PHASE 2

The Readiness Roadmap

(Quantify the ROI)

$7,500 | ~3 Weeks

We audit your capacity waste and generate the verifiable Baseline Metrics and custom ROI model. This aligns the project with your internal financial hurdle.

COMMITMENT:

Board-Ready Business Case with custom ROI targets.

PHASE 3

The Compliance Shield

(Zero Tolerance for Shadow IT)

$8–12k | ~2 Weeks

This pre-audit step maps deployment against ISO 27001 and PI requirements, providing the auditable Risk Control Matrix needed for CTO approval.

MITIGATION:

Guarantees zero data egress and regulatory compliance.

PHASE 4

Wave 1 Implementation

(Predictable Deployment and Full Ownership)

$20–30k | ~30-Day Sprint

The final fixed-scope sprint puts live workflows in your secure tenant. This targets Tier 2 Priority routines first to address the highest design liability risks.

GUARANTEE:

15% Fee Retention held until success metrics are met.

Technical Differentiators & Ownership

Proprietary Moat & Flexibility

Our moat is the Extraction Schema and Logic Trace framework—the reusable methodology developed specifically for structural engineering data complexity, ensuring faster, more accurate deployment.

Mitigation via Modularity: We separate the stable process control layer (M365/Power Automate) from the interchangeable AI execution layer (LLM API), guaranteeing data residency without vendor lock-in to a single LLM.

Zero Risk & Full Ownership

Data Residency: The solution utilizes the client's existing secure cloud tenant (Azure, AWS, or GCP) to guarantee zero data egress and satisfy regulatory compliance.

Full IP Ownership: We transfer 100% of the source code and flows to your tenant. This eliminates licensing fees from Curam-Ai, transforming the solution into a self-sustaining asset.
