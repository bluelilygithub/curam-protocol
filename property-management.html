We build a three-layer validation system to prevent costly errors:

Layer 1 - Confidence Scoring: If the AI's confidence score is below 90% on critical fields (lease end date, bond amount, rent), the document is automatically flagged for human review.

Layer 2 - Business Logic Validation: We configure rules specific to your requirements. Example: "Bond must equal 4 weeks rent in Queensland." Mismatches trigger alerts.

Layer 3 - Human Review Queue: Flagged records route to your property manager within the same workflow. Review takes 30-60 seconds to confirm or correct.

Target accuracy: 99%+ on production documents after model training. The small percentage flagged for review prevents catastrophic errors (missed renewals, incorrect bond lodgements).
