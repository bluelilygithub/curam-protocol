We build a three-layer validation system to prevent costly errors:

Layer 1 - Confidence Scoring: If the AI's confidence score is below 90% on critical fields (service codes, participant IDs, grant amounts, timestamps), the document is automatically flagged for human review.

Layer 2 - Business Logic Validation: We configure rules specific to your requirements. Examples: "Service codes must match NDIS approved codes," "Grant amounts must match approved funding," "Timestamps must be within service delivery period." Mismatches trigger alerts.

Layer 3 - Human Review Queue: Flagged records route to your compliance officer within the same workflow. Review takes 30-60 seconds to confirm or correct.

Target accuracy: 99%+ on production documents after model training. The small percentage flagged for review prevents catastrophic errors (incorrect service logs, missed grant requirements, audit failures).
