We build a three-layer validation system to prevent costly errors:

Layer 1 - Confidence Scoring: If the AI's confidence score is below 90% on critical fields (contract terms, dates, parties, client information), the document is automatically flagged for human review.

Layer 2 - Business Logic Validation: We configure rules specific to your requirements. Examples: "Contract dates must be valid," "Non-standard terms must be flagged," "Client conflicts must be checked against database." Mismatches trigger alerts.

Layer 3 - Human Review Queue: Flagged records route to your paralegal or junior associate within the same workflow. Review takes 30-60 seconds to confirm or correct.

Target accuracy: 99%+ on production documents after model training. The small percentage flagged for review prevents catastrophic errors (missed contract terms, incorrect client information, privilege breaches).

System designed to "fail safe." Extractions below 95% confidence flagged for human review. Paralegal verifies before commitment. You're eliminating typing, not eliminating oversight.
