Accounting

To arrive at a figure like $700,000, we aren't just looking at "time saved"; we are looking at the Total Loaded Cost of Production and the Opportunity Cost of Headcount.

For a firm with 50+ staff, the production floor (Mid/Juniors and Admin) represents the largest line item in the budget. Here is the mathematical breakdown of how those savings materialize across your specific staff tiers:

1. The Mid & Junior Tier (25 Staff)

This is where the bulk of the "Accounting Logic" automation happens (GL coding, inter-entity matching, basic reconciliations).

  • Time Recovery: 5 hours per week per person. (This is conservative; in many firms, juniors spend 15+ hours on manual data work).
  • Weekly Total: 25 staff × 5 hours = 125 hours/week
  • Annual Total: 125 hours × 48 weeks = 6,000 production hours
  • Cost Value: At a mid-market loaded cost (salary + super + overhead) of ~$100/hr, that is $600,000 in recovered capacity.
2. The Admin Tier (10 Staff)

This tier handles the "Document Drudgery" (filing PDFs, rename-matching, chasing missing info).

  • Time Recovery: 8 hours per week per person (1 full day of manual admin).
  • Weekly Total: 10 staff × 8 hours = 80 hours/week
  • Annual Total: 80 hours × 48 weeks = 3,840 admin hours
  • Cost Value: At a loaded cost of ~$50/hr, that is $192,000 in recovered capacity.

The "Skeptic's Adjustment"

If we add those together ($600k + $192k), we get $792,000. We round down to $700,000 to account for "implementation friction" and the fact that staff don't always fill every recovered minute with billable work.

Why this number is "Real" for a Partner:

While you don't necessarily "fire" staff to get this $700k back, the value is realized in three ways:

  1. Headcount Avoidance: You can grow your revenue by 15-20% without hiring the next 5-8 people. In the current Australian talent market, avoiding 5 hires saves you ~$500k in salaries alone, plus recruitment fees.
  2. Senior Leverage: Because the 25 Mid/Juniors are producing "Audit-Ready" work, your 15 Seniors spend less time "fixing" and more time "reviewing." If each Senior saves just 2 hours a week of correction work, that's another $150k+ of high-value time unlocked.
  3. Write-offs: Much of the manual data entry performed by juniors is often written off because it exceeds the fixed fee for the job. Automation turns those write-offs back into profit.

Summary for the Website

On the page, we frame this as "Annual Production Value." It tells the Partner: "This is the cost of the manual labor your firm is currently 'burning' on tasks that AI can do with 90% accuracy."

ROI Justification: The Production Floor Economics

For a 50+ person law firm, the $850,000+ Annual Production Value is not a hypothetical number—it is a direct calculation of recovered labor costs and increased throughput within your Junior and Admin tiers.

1. The Mathematical Breakdown
Junior & Mid-Level Associate Tier (25 Staff)

These staff members are the "engine room" of the firm, but they are often bogged down by manual document extraction and precedent searching that cannot be fully billed or leads to significant write-offs.

  • Time Recovery: 5 hours per week (conservative estimate of time spent on manual data entry and basic file prep).
  • Weekly Total: 25 Associates × 5 hours = 125 hours recovered
  • Annual Total: 125 hours × 48 weeks = 6,000 hours
  • Production Value: At a mid-market loaded cost (salary + super + overhead) of $110/hr, this equals $660,000 in recovered production capacity.
Legal Admin & Paralegal Tier (10 Staff)

This tier handles the high-volume "drudgery" of matter setup, conflict data entry, and document filing.

  • Time Recovery: 8 hours per week (1 full day recovered from manual admin tasks).
  • Weekly Total: 10 Staff × 8 hours = 80 hours recovered
  • Annual Total: 80 hours × 48 weeks = 3,840 hours
  • Production Value: At a loaded cost of $55/hr, this equals $211,200 in recovered capacity.

Combined Total

$660,000 + $211,200 = $871,200. We present this as $850,000+ to remain conservative.

2. The Senior Review "Multiplier"

While we do not include the Partners' or Seniors' hourly rates in the savings to keep the ROI realistic, the Senior Review Efficiency acts as a force multiplier for the firm's profitability.

  • Reduction in Re-work: By providing Seniors with "Audit-Ready" documents from the start, the time they spend "red-penning" basic errors or re-extracting missed terms is reduced by 40%.
  • Scale Factor: This allows your 15 Partners/Seniors to supervise a larger volume of files without increasing their own working hours, directly leading to the 20% Increased Matter Throughput.
3. Real-World Business Impact

In the current Australian legal market, a $50k investment that yields $850k in production value addresses three critical Partner concerns:

  1. Headcount Avoidance: You gain the equivalent capacity of 5.1 Full-Time Employees without the cost of recruitment, training, or additional office space.
  2. Margin Protection: On fixed-fee matters (like conveyancing or standard commercial leases), every manual hour saved is pure profit.
  3. Associate Retention: By removing the "soul-crushing" manual data entry, you reduce junior turnover—a massive hidden cost for mid-sized firms.
