Understanding Wave 1 vs. Wave 2

Phase 2 designs a phased implementation approach. This reduces risk, delivers quick wins, and manages change effectively.

Wave 1: Quick Wins

2-4 Weeks

High-impact, low-complexity workflows that deliver immediate value. Examples: Invoice processing, standard form extraction, file routing automation.

Goal: Prove the system works and build user confidence.

Wave 2: Complex Workflows

4-6 Weeks

Advanced use cases requiring custom logic or multi-step validation. Examples: Engineering schedules with variable formats, tender document analysis, ERP integration.

Goal: Scale the solution to cover your full workflow.

Phase 2 doesn't build anything. It defines what gets built, in what order, and at what cost. Implementation happens in Phase 4.

What We Need From Your Team

Phase 2 is collaborative. To build your Roadmap, we require limited but focused time from your stakeholders:

1. Document Access

30-50 representative documents (historical invoices, schedules, or forms) plus read-only access to your document storage system to understand current workflows.

2. Stakeholder Interviews

2-3 x 60-minute sessions with Subject Matter Experts (Finance, Operations, IT) to map business rules, approval workflows, and exception handling logic.

3. IT/Security Review

1 x 90-minute session with your IT Admin or CTO to map your tech stack, security requirements, and integration architecture.

4. ERP/System Context

Screen-share or demo of your current ERP data entry process to understand field mappings, validation rules, and downstream system dependencies.

Total time commitment: ~5-7 hours spread over 3 weeks. We work around your schedule.
