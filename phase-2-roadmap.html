Input vs. Output

We compare the raw PDF (Input) against what your staff actually typed into the ERP (Output). This reveals the "Human Logic Gap"—the data they fix, ignore, or invent.

We digitize the sticky notes and SOPs on your employees' desks. This captures the hidden routing rules (e.g., "If Vendor X, send to Bob") that aren't in the data.

3

We audit your security perimeter. We define exactly where the data lives, how it moves to your ERP, and map the ISO-27001 controls required to approve it.
