What About Phase 3?

Phase 3 (Compliance Shield) is optional and assessed during Phase 2.

✓ Who Needs Phase 3?

  • Large enterprises (100+ staff) with formal governance frameworks
  • Regulated industries (legal, finance, healthcare) with PI insurance requirements
  • Firms with strict security protocols (ISO 27001, SOC 2 compliance)
  • Publicly traded companies with external audit obligations

Phase 3 deliverables: ISO 27001 control mapping, PI Insurance questionnaire, Risk Control Matrix, Shadow IT inventory, pre-filled compliance questionnaires

→ Who Can Skip Phase 3?

  • Small-medium firms (<100 staff) without formal compliance frameworks
  • Firms using existing M365/Azure governance (Phase 2 validates compatibility)
  • Fast-moving deployments (Wave 1 only, no ERP integration)
  • Firms with low regulatory burden (no external audit requirements)

Most clients skip Phase 3: Phase 2 assessment determines if compliance documentation is required for your specific deployment.

Decision Point: Phase 2 will assess if Phase 3 is required for your deployment. If your firm already operates within M365/Azure governance and has no external audit obligations, you'll likely proceed directly to Phase 4 implementation.
