We build a three-layer validation system to prevent costly errors:

Layer 1 - Confidence Scoring: If the AI's confidence score is below 90% on critical fields (tariff codes, cargo weights, values, origin), the document is automatically flagged for human review.

Layer 2 - Business Logic Validation: We configure rules specific to your requirements. Examples: "Tariff codes must match ABF classifications," "Cargo weights must be within acceptable range," "Values must match invoice amounts." Mismatches trigger alerts.

Layer 3 - Human Review Queue: Flagged records route to your coordinator within the same workflow. Review takes 30-60 seconds to confirm or correct.

Target accuracy: 99%+ on production documents after model training. The small percentage flagged for review prevents catastrophic errors (incorrect tariff codes, misclassified cargo, customs fines).

System designed to "fail safe." Extractions below 95% confidence flagged for human review. Coordinators verify before submission. You're eliminating typing, not eliminating oversight.
