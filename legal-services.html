Legal Firm FAQs

Common questions from partners considering AI automation.

What about legal professional privilege and confidentiality? +
All data remains within your Microsoft 365 tenant in Australia. We use Azure AI services with encryption at rest and in transit. No client data is sent to third-party AI providers like OpenAI. We document the entire data flow for your ethics committee and comply with Law Society guidelines on technology adoption.
Does this integrate with ActionStep/LEAP/Smokeball? +
Yes. We build integrations with all major Australian practice management systems. Extracted data flows directly into your existing system via API, so staff don't need to switch platforms or learn new software.
What if the AI makes a mistake extracting contract terms? +
The system is designed to "fail safe." Any extraction with confidence below 95% is flagged for human review via Teams notification. A paralegal verifies the output before it's committed to your practice management system. You're eliminating typing, not eliminating oversight.
How long before we see ROI? +
Most legal firms see payback in 6-8 weeks. Phase 1 (Feasibility) proves the ROI exists. Phase 2 (Roadmap) quantifies it with your actual numbers.
