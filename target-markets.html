Target Markets

Document-Heavy Professional Services Firms Ready for Automation

Primary Target Market

Small to mid-sized professional services firms that are:

Document Processing Dealing with 5-10 hours per week of manual data entry tasks
Document Types Processing structured documents like invoices, schedules, transmittals, reports, and registers
Infrastructure Using Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace cloud infrastructure
Approach Looking to improve efficiency without massive IT transformation projects

Decision-Makers

Two key personas drive the decision-making process:

CTOs / IT Directors

Concerned about:

  • Security and compliance
  • System ownership and control
  • Maintenance and support
  • Technical risk and integration

Partners / Managing Directors

Need:

  • Board-ready business cases
  • ROI justification and metrics
  • Strategic planning and roadmaps
  • Risk mitigation strategies

Firm Characteristics

Ideal customers typically have:

Size 10-100 employees (small enough to feel the pain of manual work, large enough to afford $10k-50k projects)
Infrastructure Existing cloud infrastructure (Azure, AWS, or M365)
Volume Process significant volumes of standard professional documents
Compliance PI insurance and ISO 27001 compliance requirements
Systems Currently using systems like Xero, MYOB, or QuickBooks for accounting
Mindset Skeptical of AI but open to proof-of-concept approach

Pain Points They're Experiencing

Common challenges that drive firms to seek automation:

Wasted Billable Hours Junior staff spending hours on data entry instead of billable work
Manual Compilation Time-consuming manual compilation of registers and reports
Processing Bottlenecks Invoice and document processing creating workflow delays
Data Extraction Manual extraction of data from PDFs for reporting and analysis
Human Error Risk Risk of costly mistakes in data transcription
Modernization Pressure Pressure to modernize but fear of failed AI implementations

Why These Industries?

They all share common characteristics that make them ideal candidates for document automation:

High Document Volumes Structured data in repetitive document formats
Expensive Staff Time High-cost staff time spent on low-value data entry
Compliance Requirements Need for audit trails, accuracy, and regulatory compliance
Existing Infrastructure Cloud/on-premise infrastructure already in place

Sweet Spot Customer Profile

The ideal customer has:

  • 50+ hours/month spent on manual document processing
  • $80k+/year salary cost for admin/junior staff doing data entry
  • Existing cloud tools but manually bridging gaps between systems
  • Privacy/security sensitive (your private cloud positioning wins here)
  • Risk-averse (your phased approach with exits is perfect)
