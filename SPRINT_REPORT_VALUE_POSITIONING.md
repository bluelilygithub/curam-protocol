## Psychological Triggers Added

### ✅ **Specificity = Credibility**
- "15 of YOUR documents"
- "50-person engineering firm"
- "$888k" (not "~$900k")

### ✅ **Risk Reversal**
- "Most firms recover the $1,500 in the first week"
- "590x ROI"
- Shows failure scenarios (adoption rates)

### ✅ **Customization = Value**
- "YOUR numbers" (mentioned 5 times)
- "YOUR documents" (mentioned 3 times)
- "Based on YOUR data"

### ✅ **Social Proof (Indirect)**
- "Most clients see..."
- "Board-Ready" (implies others have used this)

### ✅ **Urgency/Scarcity (Subtle)**
- "48-hour turnaround" (fast, limited capacity implied)
- "First week of implementation" (quick results)
