Security & Compliance

Is the Phase 2 'Security Readiness Review' a formal cybersecurity audit?

+

No. Our review is a targeted investigation for the AI implementation. It verifies permission scoping and zero-egress integrity to ensure the environment is safe for deployment. It provides the evidence your actual auditor will need but doesn't replace your annual independent certification.

Does all data stay within our M365/Azure environment?

+

Yes. 100% of processing happens inside your tenant. We configure zero-egress compliance from day one. You control the keys, you control the data.

Where is data processed? Can you guarantee Australian data residency?

+

All processing occurs exclusively within your Azure/M365 cloud tenant. If you specify Australian data residency (Azure Australia East), we configure the solution to respect that. No data is routed outside your secure environment.

Engineering & Built Environment

Do you have examples specific to civil/structural engineering?

+

Yes. Our models handle Concrete Test Reports (lot numbers, slump, strength), Beam Schedules (dimensions, steel grade), and Drawing Registers (revisions, dates). We pre-train on technical jargon, not just generic text.

How do you ensure AI didn't swap a 'UB' for a 'UC' on a schedule?

+

Every extraction includes source verification. The AI copies exactly what is in the PDF—it doesn't interpret. If a scan is unclear, it flags a confidence score <90% for human review rather than guessing.

Can this handle BIM schedules and Revit exports?

+

Yes. We handle window/door schedules and drawing registers exported from Revit or ArchiCAD, maintaining revision control and auto-populating your internal registers. Typical savings: 8-12 hours per week.
