Accuracy & Risk Management

How can AI make "judgment-based" calls?

+

Our AI doesn't replace professional judgment; it mimics the "first-pass" logic of a senior associate. We use The Protocol™ to set specific confidence thresholds. If the AI is 99% sure a document meets a compliance rule, it proceeds; if it encounters an edge case requiring nuance, it flags it for human review. This is Human-in-the-loop automation, not "Set-and-forget."

How do you guarantee accuracy and prevent AI hallucinations?

+

We use a three-layer validation system: Grounding (AI only extracts from your documents), Confidence Scoring (flags items scoring <90% for human review), and ERP Cross-Checks (validation against vendor master lists/project codes).

What if the AI achieves 95% accuracy, but failure is in our most critical documents?

+

This is why Phase 1 is mandatory. Our 90% pass/fail guarantee is measured only against YOUR verified answer key for high-stakes documents. If it can't handle them, we issue a full refund immediately.

What if the AI makes a costly mistake in production?

+

Our validation layers flag errors before they reach your ERP. Furthermore, we carry Professional Indemnity insurance which covers errors made by the AI implementation. Details are provided during the contract phase.

How much time will my team spend on human review?

+

Typically 5–15 minutes per day reviewing the <10% of documents the AI flags. This is high-value risk mitigation, not cleanup.
