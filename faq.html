Security & Compliance

▶

How do you address the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)?

+

Through Data Sovereignty. The Protocol is deployed inside your own Azure, AWS, or GCP tenant. Data never leaves your environment or the Australian jurisdiction.

Can we test the AI if our documents contain highly sensitive PII?

+

Yes. We recommend a Redacted Feasibility Sprint. You black out PII on 15 sample documents before sending them to us to prove accuracy without exposing live data.

Is the Phase 2 'Security Readiness Review' a formal audit?

+

No. It is an AI Readiness Review investigating Permission Scoping and API Integrity. It provides evidence your actual auditor will need.

Do you meet SOC2, ISO, and Australian Privacy Principles (APP)?

+

Yes. By deploying internally, we inherit your posture. We provide configuration documentation for your SOC2/ISO auditors in Phase 2.

Does all data stay within our environment?

+

Yes. 100% of processing happens in your tenant. No data leaves your control. No data is used to train public models.

How do you guarantee ISO 27001 compliance?

+

We map deployment against ISO 27001 controls in Phase 3, creating data flow diagrams and access control matrices.

Where is data processed?

+

Exclusively within your specified region (e.g., Azure Australia East). We guarantee Australian data residency.

Will this require opening new firewall ports?

+

No. Zero new firewall ports. Integration uses outbound-only communication via secure APIs already authorized.

What is the protocol for legacy on-premise servers?

+

We use Microsoft's Data Gateway—a secure connector running inside your firewall with no inbound ports needed.

Accuracy & Risk Management

▶

How can AI make "judgment-based" calls?

+

Our AI mimics senior-associate logic. We use The Protocol™ to set specific confidence thresholds. Anything falling below 90% confidence is flagged for human review.

How accurate is the digitization of handwritten site logs?

+

Accuracy depends on handwriting quality. Realistically, we may not be 100% accurate on handwritten docs, which is why we implement Human-in-the-Loop verification.

How do you guarantee accuracy and prevent AI hallucinations?

+

Through a three-layer system: Grounding (AI only extracts from your documents), Confidence Scoring, and ERP Cross-Checks.

What if the AI fails on our most critical documents?

+

This is why Phase 1 is mandatory. If we can't hit 90% accuracy on your documents, you get a full refund immediately.

What if the AI makes a costly mistake in production?

+

Validation layers flag errors before they reach your ERP. Furthermore, we carry Professional Indemnity insurance that covers our AI implementations.

How much time will my team spend on human review?

+

Typically 5–15 minutes per day reviewing the <10% of documents the AI flags as low-confidence.
